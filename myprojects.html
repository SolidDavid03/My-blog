San Raffaele chatbot

This chatbot was built using FlowiseAI for the hospital "San Raffaele" located in Milan and its main purpose is to provide clinical consulting to other less experienced doctors in order to guide them to treat an infected Covid-19 patient. This works by being provided some clinical values by the doctor, these are mandatory to provide a consulting on how to treat the patient.
